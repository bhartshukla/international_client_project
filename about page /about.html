About Molmet Biotech

A Discovery-Driven Biotech Research and Innovation Hub

Explore Our Vision Collaborate With Us

Molmet Biotech Research Pvt Ltd was founded with a clear vision to unlock the untapped potential of biological systems and translate it into meaningful scientific and industrial outcomes. The name Molmet reflects our core philosophy — Molecules and Metabolites — representing our focus on discovering and harnessing bioactive compounds derived from microbial, plant, and natural sources. We operate at the intersection of discovery, applied research, and collaboration, building a platform that connects biodiversity with innovation and real-world application.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized hub for microbial discovery and bioactive molecule innovation, driven by systematic bioprospecting and the development of a robust microbial biobank — contributing to science, sustainability, and human well-being.

Explore Our Mission

Our Mission

Explore Microbial Ecosystems To explore underutilized microbial ecosystems — tapping into Earth's most diverse and undercharted biological reservoirs.
Discover Bioactive Molecules To discover novel bioactive molecules and metabolites with scientific, medical, and industrial significance.
Build Biological Assets To build valuable biological and process-driven assets through systematic collection, preservation, and data generation.
Enable Collaborative Innovation To enable collaborative innovation across industries — bridging scientific discovery with real-world application and impact.

Who We Are

Molmet Biotech is an applied research-driven organization focused on microbial bioprospecting, molecule discovery, and translational biotechnology.

We integrate:

Field-based exploration
Laboratory-driven discovery
Industry-aligned research execution

We are not a conventional laboratory — we are a discovery platform transforming biodiversity into bioactive innovation.

Scientific Approach

Our Scientific Approach

Our work follows a structured and integrated pathway:

01
Bioprospecting across diverse ecosystems Field collection across underexplored environments to access rare microbial diversity.
02
Isolation and characterization of microbial strains Systematic isolation followed by morphological and molecular characterization of novel strains.
03
Screening for bioactive molecules and metabolites High-throughput activity screening against antimicrobial, anticancer, and enzyme targets.
04
In-vitro validation and functional evaluation Controlled laboratory models to validate biological activity and selectivity profiles.
05
Translation through collaboration Partnering with industry and institutions to translate validated leads into development candidates.

Our Team

Our Leadership

Founder & Scientific Director
Founder
Mayank Tenguria
Founder & Scientific Director, Molmet Biotech Research Pvt Ltd

Mayank Tenguria is a researcher and biopreneur with a strong focus on microbial bioprospecting, bioactive molecule & metabolite discovery, and applied biotechnology. He conceptualized Molmet with a vision to explore biological diversity and translate it into meaningful scientific, industrial, and societal solutions through discovery-driven approaches.

His work integrates microbiology, molecular biology, cell-based systems, nanotechnology, and bioanalytical techniques to generate application-oriented solutions. He has led multiple research initiatives spanning microbial discovery, pharmacological screening, metabolite analysis, and nanoparticle-based systems, consistently bridging discovery with real-world applications.

His vision is to build Molmet into a globally recognized discovery platform driven by bioprospecting, biological asset development, and collaborative innovation.

Executive Director
Executive Director
Dr. Dharmendra Singh Khichi
Executive Director, Agriculture & Agri-Biotech Strategy

Dr. Khichi brings extensive experience in agriculture R&D, product development, and deployment of bio-based solutions. His work focuses on aligning scientific innovation with sustainable agricultural practices through biofertilizers, biopesticides, functional biochemical formulations and biocontrol strategies.

His efforts have contributed to improving agricultural practices and supporting farming communities across diverse regions.

He is committed to advancing sustainable agri-biotech solutions by integrating biological innovation with practical field-level impact.

Scientist & Consultant
Scientist & Consultant
Dr. Rajnandani Katariya

Dr. Katariya is a scientist and consultant with expertise in nanotechnology, cell culture systems, and translational biomedical research. Her work includes anticancer activity evaluation, cytotoxicity studies, and molecular oncology investigations using advanced in-vitro models.

Dr. Katariya contributes to the design and execution of bioactivity screening and cell-based studies, supporting the discovery and evaluation of novel bioactive molecules within Molmet's research and collaboration programs.

Scientist & Bio-Asset Manager
Scientist & Bio-Asset Manager
Ayush Kushwaha
M.Sc. Microbiology — Barkatullah University

Ayush specializes in microbial isolation, molecular identification, and bioactivity screening, along with metabolite analysis using analytical techniques. He contributes to the development, organization, and maintenance of Molmet's biological asset platform.

Scientist & Consultant
Scientist & Consultant
