About Molmet Biotech

A Discovery-Driven Biotech Research and Innovation Hub

Molmet Biotech Research Pvt Ltd was founded with a clear vision - to unlock the untapped potential of biological systems and translate it into meaningful scientific and industrial solutions.

The name Molmet reflects our core philosophy - Molecules and Metabolites - representing our focus on discovering, understanding, and harnessing bioactive compounds derived from microbial, plant, and natural sources. We operate at the intersection of discovery, applied research, and collaboration, building a platform that connects biodiversity with innovation and real-world impact.

Our Scientific Approach

Our work follows a structured and integrated discovery pathway.

Bioprospecting from geographically diverse ecosystemsField collection across underexplored environments to access rare microbial diversity.

Our Leadership

Founder & Scientific Director
Founder
Mayank Tenguria
Founder & Scientific Director, Molmet Biotech Research Pvt Ltd

Mayank Tenguria is a researcher and biopreneur with a strong focus on microbial bioprospecting, bioactive molecule and metabolite discovery, and applied biotechnology. He conceptualized and established Molmet Biotech with a clear vision to explore biological diversity and translate it into meaningful scientific, industrial, and societal solutions through the discovery of novel molecules and metabolites.

His work centers on exploring underutilized microbial ecosystems and natural resources, integrating classical and modern biotechnological approaches — including microbiology, molecular biology, cell culture systems, nanotechnology, and bioanalytical techniques — to generate scientifically robust and application-oriented outcomes.

He has led multiple research initiatives spanning microbial discovery, pharmacological screening, metabolite analysis, and nanoparticle-based systems, with a consistent focus on bridging discovery with real-world applications.

His vision is to build Molmet into a globally recognized discovery platform for bioactive molecules and metabolites, driven by bioprospecting, biological asset development, and collaborative innovation.

Ayush Kushwaha
