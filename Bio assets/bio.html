At Molmet Biotech, we are building an evolving repository of microbial strains, metabolite systems, and discovery-oriented biological resources generated through systematic bioprospecting and experimental investigation. These resources are structured to support scientific exploration, translational research, and application-focused innovation across multiple biotechnology domains.

Molmet BioBank
– Microbial Culture Collection

Our microbial culture collection comprises geographically distinct and functionally relevant isolates sourced from diverse and underexplored ecological environments.

Each strain is systematically preserved, characterized, and integrated into ongoing discovery and validation programs.

The collection is continuously expanding through active bioprospecting initiatives and serves as a long-term foundation for collaborative research and biological exploration.

Metabolite Production Platform

Our metabolite production platform focuses on directing microbial systems toward targeted production of biologically active molecules through optimized cultivation and bioprocess strategies.

We investigate how environmental conditions, nutrient systems, and fermentation parameters influence metabolite expression, enabling controlled and reproducible production workflows.

Many microbial strains possess the capacity to express multiple metabolites under different growth conditions. Our optimization strategies are designed to selectively enhance desired functional outputs.

These assets are not just collections — they are engineered opportunities for innovation and value creation, deployable for research and industry.

One microbial system — multiple functional possibilities directed toward defined outcomes.

Discovery Collections
