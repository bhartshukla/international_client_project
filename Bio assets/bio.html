Microbial Diversity & Bioactive Potential

Biological Asset Platform

A Curated Repository of Microbial Diversity and Bioactive Potential for Discovery Innovation and Application

At Molmet Biotech, we are building a structured and expanding biological asset platform derived from systematic bioprospecting and discovery efforts. Our assets include microbial strains, metabolite production systems, and discovery-driven collections that form the foundation for innovation, collaboration, and future translational applications. This platform is designed to continuously generate, refine, and deploy biological assets for scalable, application-driven outcomes.

Molmet BioBank – Microbial Culture Collection

Our Microbial Culture Library comprises isolates collected from diverse and underexplored ecological niches, particularly across Central India and other unique environments. Each strain is systematically isolated, preserved, and catalogued to support discovery-driven research and high-value collaborative innovation. The collection is designed for reproducibility, rapid access, and seamless integration into discovery and development pipelines.

Each isolate is more than a strain — it is a characterized entry point into novel biology and functional innovation.

Metabolite Production Platform

Metabolite Production Platform

Our Metabolite Production Platform focuses on optimizing microbial systems for targeted production of bioactive molecules through controlled fermentation and bioprocess strategies. Each asset is characterized, validated, and optimized for functional output and scalability.

Many microbial strains possess the ability to produce multiple metabolites depending on environmental and cultivation conditions. At Molmet, we design and optimize these conditions to selectively enhance the production of desired compounds.

These assets are not just collections — they are engineered opportunities for innovation and value creation, deployable for research and industry.

One strain, multiple possibilities — optimized for targeted & scalable outcomes.

Capabilities

Media optimization for targeted metabolite expression
Bioprocess parameter tuning (pH, temperature, nutrients, aeration)
Strain-specific fermentation strategies for optimal pathway activation
Yield enhancement and reproducibility
Multi-metabolite differentiation from single strains

Discovery Collections

Discovery Collections

Our Discovery Collections represent curated sets of microbial isolates and associated extracts identified through ongoing bioprospecting and screening programs.

These collections are organized based on biological activity, ecological origin, and research relevance, forming a dynamic resource for targeted discovery initiatives.

These dynamic collections are continuously refined and expanded to support focused exploration of bioactive potential across diverse application areas.
