Bioprospecting

Microbial Bioprospecting from Underexplored Ecosystems

India's diverse ecological landscapes — particularly Central India — represent a vast and largely untapped reservoir of microbial diversity.

At Molmet, we systematically explore these environments to isolate and identify microorganisms with unique metabolic capabilities that hold potential for future biotechnological innovation.

Turning unexplored ecosystems directly into a continuous pipeline of novel microbial assets and proprietary value.

Molecule Discovery

Molecule & Metabolite Discovery Platform

Our discovery platform is engineered to systematically identify and validate bioactive metabolites from novel microbial isolates. We integrate targeted screening, analytical profiling, and rigorous functional validation to characterize molecules with defined biological activity. Each discovery is advanced through structured pipelines toward pharmaceutical, agricultural, environmental, and industrial applications.

Our focus is clear — move from molecule identification to application-ready scalable outcomes with precision and purpose.

From microbes to molecules — we define the drivers of biological activity.

Discovery Pipeline

From Ecosystem to Validated Lead

A fully integrated, five-stage discovery pipeline — engineered for scientific rigor at every transition from sample to validated bioactive candidate.

Isolation

Systematic sampling and isolation of novel microbial strains from target ecosystems.

Cultivation

Controlled cultivation under optimized conditions to maximize growth and metabolite expression.

Extraction

Extraction and fractionation of bioactive metabolites for downstream screening.

Screening

In-vitro assays against antimicrobial, anticancer, enzymatic, and industrial activity targets.

Validation

Confirmed bioactive leads ready for collaborative development and translation.

Bioactivity & Validation

Bioactivity Screening & Functional Validation

We employ targeted in-vitro and application-oriented assays to evaluate biological activity and validate the functional potential of discovered metabolites and microbial systems. We prioritize function-led screening to identify candidates with clear translational and industrial applicability. Each validated hit is positioned for downstream development across pharmaceutical, agricultural, environmental, and industrial biotechnology pipelines. Our approach ensures that discovery is aligned with real-world biological relevance.

Strain Improvement

Strain Improvement & Optimization

Discovery does not end at identification. We work on optimizing microbial strains and culture conditions to enhance metabolite yield, improve reproducibility, and enable targeted production of desired bioactive compounds.

Our approach integrates adaptive evolution, process optimization, and controlled cultivation strategies to unlock full production potential. We ensure consistent performance under scalable conditions, enabling seamless transition to downstream development and commercialization.

Each optimized strain is aligned with defined application goals across pharmaceutical and industrial biotechnology.

We don't just discover — we improve.

A single microbial strain can express multiple metabolites — our focus is to direct that potential toward defined outcomes.

Molmet BioBank

Molmet BioBank — Microbial Culture Library

Every strain we isolate becomes a long-term asset. We are actively building a curated and expanding repository of microbial strains sourced from diverse ecological niches. This culture library represents a growing biological asset base for discovery, screening, and collaborative innovation.

Each strain is systematically preserved, characterized, and positioned as a reusable asset within our discovery ecosystem. This evolving repository is not just a collection — it is our proprietary foundation for continuous innovation and long-term competitive advantage.

A strategic microbial bank built to fuel high-value discovery pipelines.

Application Potential

Application Potential
