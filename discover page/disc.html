03 — Strain Engineering

Strain Engineering & Optimization

Discovery doesn't end with isolation. Molmet's strain engineering capabilities maximize the potential of each lead — optimizing yield, stability, and scalability for downstream development.

Genetic Characterization

Comprehensive molecular profiling — 16S rRNA, ITS sequencing, and phylogenetic analysis — establishing the definitive identity and novelty of each isolate.

Culture Optimization

Systematic optimization of growth conditions, media formulations, and fermentation parameters to maximize metabolite production and strain stability.

Yield Enhancement

Targeted improvement of biosynthetic output through optimized induction strategies, elicitor screening, and process engineering.

Cryopreservation & Banking

Long-term strain preservation with validated viability protocols ensuring reproducibility across research programs and future collaboration.

Biosafety Classification

Rigorous assessment and documentation of biosafety levels, supporting compliant integration into partner pipelines and regulatory environments.

Pipeline Integration

Optimized strains delivered as collaboration-ready assets — with full characterization data packages for seamless integration into partner workflows.
