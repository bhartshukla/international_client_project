Microbial Bioprospecting from Underexplored Ecosystems

India's diverse ecological landscapes — particularly Central India — represent a largely untapped reservoir of microbial diversity. We systematically explore these environments to isolate and characterize microorganisms with unique metabolic capabilities.

Environmental sampling across soil, water & niche habitats
Isolation of rare and novel microbial strains
Ecological and functional screening programs
Preservation and cataloguing of all isolates

"Every ecosystem holds untapped biochemical potential — we aim to decode it."

Bioactivity & Validation

Bioactivity Screening & Functional Validation

We employ a range of in-vitro models to evaluate biological activity and validate the functional potential of discovered metabolites — keeping it scientific and outcome-oriented.

Antimicrobial Assays

MIC determination and zone-of-inhibition studies against pathogenic bacteria and fungi.

Anticancer Screening

Cell-line based cytotoxicity assays measuring antiproliferative potential of extracts.

Enzyme Inhibition

Targeted enzyme inhibition studies for metabolic and therapeutic target validation.

Cytotoxicity Models

Selectivity index determination using standardized mammalian cell-line models.
