Discovery at Molmet

Transforming Microbial Diversity into Bioactive Innovation

Explore the Platform Discovery Pipeline

Molmet Biotech is focused on discovering novel microorganisms, bioactive molecules, and functional metabolites from underexplored biological systems. Through integrated bioprospecting, screening, and validation workflows, we build discovery pipelines aligned with pharmaceutical, agricultural, industrial, and environmental innovation.

Bioprospecting

Microbial Bioprospecting from Underexplored Ecosystems

India's diverse ecosystems — particularly Central India — represent a significant and underexplored source of microbial diversity.

At Molmet, we systematically isolate and investigate microorganisms with unique metabolic capabilities and application potential.

Turning unexplored ecosystems directly into a continuous pipeline of novel microbial assets and discovery opportunities.

Strain Improvement

Strain Improvement & Optimization

Discovery extends beyond identification. We optimize microbial strains and cultivation systems to improve metabolite yield, reproducibility, and targeted functional output.

Our workflows integrate controlled fermentation strategies, media optimization, and process refinement to support scalable and application-oriented production.

Each optimized strain is aligned with defined application goals across pharmaceutical and industrial biotechnology.

We don't just discover — we improve.

A single microbial strain can express multiple metabolites — our focus is directing that potential toward defined outcomes.

Molmet BioBank

Molmet BioBank — Microbial Culture Library

Every isolate within our repository is treated as a long-term biological asset.

We are actively building a curated microbial culture collection comprising geographically distinct, functionally active, and industrially relevant strains sourced through ongoing bioprospecting programs.

Each strain is systematically preserved, characterized, and integrated into discovery and validation workflows.

Molmet BioBank is a growing biological asset built to fuel high-value discovery pipelines of current need.

A strategic microbial repository designed to support long-term discovery and collaborative innovation.
