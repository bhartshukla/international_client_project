What We Offer

What We Offer

Our core strength lies in combining discovery, experimentation, and biological assets into a unified, execution driven platform:

01
Access to underexplored microbial ecosystems Bioprospecting capabilities across India's diverse ecological zones — extreme habitats, endemic niches, and undersampled environments rarely reached by conventional programs.
02
Established bioprospecting workflows Optimized, reproducible pipelines from field sampling through bioactivity screening — built for reliability and scientific rigor at every step.
03
Integrated discovery-to-validation capabilities End-to-end in-house capabilities that reduce handoff complexity, compress timelines, and maintain scientific continuity from first isolate to validated lead.
04
Biological asset platform (strains, extracts, discovery collections) An expanding, curated repository of characterized microbial strains, crude extracts, and partially purified metabolites — ready for targeted screening or licensing discussions.
05
Strong experimental execution with flexibility Agile research execution that adapts to evolving hypotheses, regulatory needs, and partner-specific requirements without sacrificing scientific depth.

We enable partners to move from biological exploration to validated, application-ready discovery.
